PASTOR STEPHEN TONG SYMPOSIUM

We are cosponsering Paster Stephen Tong’s upcoming Symposium at Brown. Reverend Dr. Tong is widely regarded as one of the most influential evangelists and theologians of our time. We are thrilled to invite you to participate in this significant event. It presents a remarkable opportunity to invigorate the Christian Community at Brown University while sharing the message of Jesus Christ with others. We envision this symposium as a unifying occasion, bringing together all Christian fellowship groups to support and engage in this momentous gathering.
